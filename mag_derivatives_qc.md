## Reading Guide

| Derivative | Colour map | What to look for |
|---|---|---|
| **FVD** | RdBu (diverging) | Blue = field intensifying upward (buried source top); Red = field weakening |
| **THG** | magma | Bright ridges = lithological contacts and fault traces |
| **AS** | magma | Amplitude of source bodies — position-independent of inducing field inclination |
| **TDR** | RdBu (diverging) | **Zero contour (white) = edge of magnetic body** |
| **THDR** | magma | Sharpest contact/edge detector; peaks over body margins |
| **SVD** | RdBu (diverging) | Enhances shallow, short-wavelength anomalies; suppresses broad regional field |
